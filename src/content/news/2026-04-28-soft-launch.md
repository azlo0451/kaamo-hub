The Kaamo Club is online at [kaamo.club](https://kaamo.club). It's the new
front door to the *Galaxy on Fire* community — a single landing point for
the Discord, the subreddit, and the wiki.gg page, with self-hosted
services arriving over the coming months.

This is a **soft launch**, and the build is happening in the open. The
[roadmap](https://github.com/azlo0451/kaamo-hub/blob/main/docs/roadmap.md)
is public, and progress is tracked on the
[project board](https://github.com/users/azlo0451/projects/3).

**Live today**

- [Bays](/bays) — links to Discord, the subreddit, the wiki.gg, and (soon) on-station services
- [News](/news) + [RSS](/rss.xml) — release notes and incident notices land here
- [About](/about), [Rules](/rules), [Privacy](/privacy) / [Datenschutz](/datenschutz)

**Coming next**

- A native wiki at `wiki.kaamo.club` — Phase 2
- A community file archive at `files.kaamo.club` — Phase 3
- Then a gallery, comments, and a Matrix room embed

The hub is run by one developer and a small admin team, on a homelab in
Germany. Found a bug or missing link? Open an issue on
[GitHub](https://github.com/azlo0451/kaamo-hub/issues), or hop into one
of the rooms linked on the bays page.

See you out there, pilot.
